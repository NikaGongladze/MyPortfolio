About Project

Chip In helps you save up money with your friends and family for common goals. You can create and manage groups and tasks in your groups.

Chip In is a user-friendly app/website designed to make saving money simple and efficient for friends and family. It offers a unique approach to group saving by allowing users to create groups, set specific saving goals (agendas), and add tasks within the group that help them achieve their financial targets. Whether you're planning a group vacation, pooling money for a gift, or simply saving for a big event, Chip In ensures everyone can contribute effortlessly toward a common goal.

1 --- Group Creation and Agenda Setting: Users can create groups with friends, family, or any chosen individuals. Within these groups, they can define a specific agenda (saving goal) such as a vacation, a gift, or any event that requires pooled funds.

2 --- Task Creation and Contribution: Each group member can create and add tasks to the group. Tasks can be personalized to suit the saving agenda and allow each member to have an active role in contributing to the group’s goal.

3 --- Easy and Fast Saving with Credit Cards: Chip In allows users to contribute easily and quickly with a seamless integration of credit card payments, making it simple to fund the group’s goals without barriers.
